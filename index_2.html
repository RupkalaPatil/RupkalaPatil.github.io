// About

Hello, I'm Rupkala.

I'm a senior data & analytics transformation leader based in Dubai, with 20+ years across GCC and global markets in automotive, retail, healthcare, telecom, and financial services. I've led enterprise BI modernization and data strategy, delivered data products such as Customer 360 and Vehicle 360, and standardized 300+ KPIs across 21+ automotive brands — recognized along the way with multiple delivery and mentorship awards.

Most recently I managed data science and data engineering operations at Al Futtaim Automotive, building governed Databricks and Power BI platforms for CXO-level decision-making. I'm currently adding Microsoft Fabric to my toolkit (preparing for DP-600), and I care about trusted metrics, governed data, and dashboards leaders actually use.
